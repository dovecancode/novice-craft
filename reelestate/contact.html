Nurturing Growth and Sustainability

Farming has long been an integral part of the Philippines' cultural heritage and economic landscape. With its favorable climate, diverse landscapes, and rich biodiversity, the archipelago has been blessed with the resources needed to support various agricultural activities.

Born and raised in a farming community in Nueva Ecija, After obtaining a degree in computer science I ventured into the bustling tech industry, working on software development projects. However, the allure of rural roots eventually led me to contemplate a unique fusion of this two passions: farming and technology.
